Join us for the first annual TradeWinds Arts Festival!

This three-day event will feature local musicians, artisans, and chefs coming together to provide the best the island has to offer.

Bring the kids!!

We will have activities for the kids all day Saturday and Sunday including:

  • Face painting
  • Puppet making
  • Puppet shows
  • Dancing
  • Drumming
  • Crafts
  • Free snacks
  • And more

See the schedule page for more details!

The festival starts on Friday night with a fundraising dinner and concert for the Island Arts Foundation. Enjoy a five-course dinner prepared by Chef Lela Rosen, a silent auction, and entertainment from local musicians. Local celebrities will be on hand to host this gala event. Tickets are available starting September 1.

Saturday's events feature fun for the entire family. Join us in the TradeWinds Emporium for classes on jewelry making, quilting, and mosaics. Learn to cook some of Chef Rosen's favorite dishes in the TradeWinds cafe. Sample the wares of dozens of local artists outdoors in the TradeWinds courtyard. Kids can enjoy puppet making, face painting, and tribal drumming activities.

On Sunday, local artists continue to exhibit their work. The Cafe will offer a jazz brunch, and an island dance troupe will perform each hour on the porch overlooking the courtyard.

So, mark your calendars so that you don't miss out. This is your chance to support the Island Arts Foundation and your local island artists while enjoy...

Great fun!

Great food!

Great music!
