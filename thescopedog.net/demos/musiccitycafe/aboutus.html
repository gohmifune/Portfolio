At one of Nashville, TN's premier cafeteria style restaraunts, we pride ourselves on our ability to serve delicious, nutritous, comfort-style foods. All across a range of types of cusines from Nashville's cultural history.

Founded in 1942, Bill Wallace, our founder, and original chef wanted a place for returning soldiers and their families to each good food at an economical price.

Over the years, more than the millitary have enjoyed the fruits of Mr. Wallace's labor. At almost 70 years, and through three generations of the Walace family, we at Music City Buffet has helped feed the fair city of Nashville, TN.
